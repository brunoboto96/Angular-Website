About Us

The Story of MEF

The transitional housing project is a non-profit social charitable enterprise that serves the home counties of Bedfordshire, Hertfordshire and Northamptonshire. The founder Eve Ricketts, who herself was indirectly exposed to domestic violence via the relationship of her parents, is qualified within the area of domestic violence due to her unrelentless commitment to meeting the needs of DV victims and through the numerous of educational studies she has taken.. Eve has worked in several refuges and shelter homes for women fleeing abuse, and has served on the board of trustees for Women’s Aid. Eve is currently doing her doctorate on domestic violence, specializing in how the aspects of witnessing domestic violence affects our young male boys.
